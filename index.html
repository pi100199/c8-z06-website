Chevrolet Corvette C8 Z06

Overview

The 2023 Chevrolet Corvette C8 Z06 is a no-compromise American supercar designed to dominate both the track and the streets. With a high-revving naturally aspirated engine, track-honed suspension, and aggressive aerodynamics, it's a vehicle born from racing DNA.

Design & Aerodynamics

Every curve and vent of the Z06 serves a purpose. The wider body accommodates larger tires and enhanced cooling, while the optional Z07 package adds a carbon rear wing and ground-hugging splitters for maximum downforce and grip.

Key Features

  • Optional carbon fiber wheels
  • Magnetic Ride Control 4.0
  • Z07 performance package
  • Quad-center exhaust tips
  • Brembo carbon-ceramic brakes
  • Multiple drive modes

Race-Proven Technology

The C8 Z06 benefits directly from Corvette Racing’s experience. The flat-plane crank V8 revs to 8,600 rpm, and the track telemetry system allows enthusiasts to analyze every second of lap time.
