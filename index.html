Chevrolet Corvette C8 Z06

Overview

The 2023 Chevrolet Corvette C8 Z06 redefines the American supercar, featuring a flat-plane crank 5.5L naturally aspirated V8 engine inspired directly by Corvette Racing's C8.R program. Designed to deliver a track-honed experience on public roads, the Z06 blends performance, precision, and exotic car presence. With a mid-engine layout for balance and a head-turning design, it's built not just to impress — but to dominate.
