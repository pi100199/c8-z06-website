Chevrolet Corvette C8 Z06

Overview

The 2023 Chevrolet Corvette C8 Z06 redefines the American supercar, featuring a flat-plane crank 5.5L naturally aspirated V8 engine inspired directly by Corvette Racing's C8.R program. Designed to deliver a track-honed experience on public roads, the Z06 blends performance, precision, and exotic car presence. With a mid-engine layout for balance and a head-turning design, it's built not just to impress — but to dominate.

Design & Aerodynamics

Form meets function with the Z06's aerodynamic bodywork. Wider fenders, front splitter, rear diffuser, and available carbon-fiber rear wing all contribute to downforce and high-speed stability. Cooling ducts, underbody airflow management, and removable front fascia inserts allow for precise track tuning. Every line, vent, and fin exists for a reason — to keep this beast glued to the pavement at speed.

Key Features

  • Optional carbon-fiber wheels (saving ~41 lbs of unsprung mass)
  • Performance Data Recorder (PDR) with telemetry & lap capture
  • Adjustable traction and performance settings via Drive Mode Selector
  • Brembo® carbon-ceramic brakes (Z07 package)

  • Electronic Limited Slip Differential (eLSD)
  • 12-inch digital instrument cluster with performance layouts
  • Magnetic Ride Control 4.0 with 1,000+ adjustments per second

Race-Proven Technology

Born from the track, the C8 Z06 was developed alongside the Corvette C8.R endurance race car. Its LT6 engine is hand-built in a clean-room environment, using titanium connecting rods and a lightweight forged crankshaft. With an aggressive intake and dry sump lubrication system, it's made for sustained high-speed track use. Paired with Michelin Pilot Sport Cup 2 R ZP tires in the Z07 package, the Z06 grips like no other street-legal Corvette before it.
