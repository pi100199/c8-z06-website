Chevrolet Corvette C8 Z06

Overview

The 2023 Chevrolet Corvette C8 Z06 is a no-compromise American supercar. Built to rival the best from Europe, it features a mid-engine platform, track-honed chassis, and an all-new 5.5L flat-plane crank V8 that screams to 8,600 RPM. This is not just a faster Corvette—this is a revolutionary statement of performance, design, and racing pedigree.

Design & Aerodynamics

Every curve and vent of the Z06 serves a purpose. Its aggressive front splitter, rear wing, and side vents are not just for show—they provide aerodynamic efficiency and cooling for the brakes and engine. The optional Z07 Performance Package enhances downforce with a carbon fiber aero kit for better lap times.

Key Features

  • Optional carbon fiber wheels (ultra-lightweight)
  • Magnetic Ride Control 4.0 adaptive suspension
  • Available Z07 performance package with upgraded aero and Michelin Cup 2 R tires
  • Quad-center exhaust tips for unique sound profile
  • 14.6” front and 15” rear Brembo carbon-ceramic brakes (Z07)
  • Multiple drive modes including Weather, Tour, Sport, Track, and MyMode

Race-Proven Technology

The C8 Z06 benefits directly from Corvette Racing’s experience. The LT6 engine is based on the same architecture used in the C8.R race car. With a dry sump oil system, forged internals, titanium intake valves, and a lightning-fast dual-clutch gearbox, the Z06 is built to perform on the street and dominate on track.
